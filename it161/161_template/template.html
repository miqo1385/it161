Roberto Bolaño

(1953-2003) Chilean narrator and poet, he is the author of storybooks,

novels and non-fiction books. He is considered one of the most

important figures in contemporary Spanish literature. he was

awarded, among otp, with the Romulo Gallego, Salambo, Altazor,

National Book Critics Circle Award and City of Barcelona award
